🗺️ Your Itinerary

1
Day 1 - Shinjuku
📍 View Shinjuku, Tokyo on Map

🌅Morning

Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building— Free observation decks offer panoramic city views. Spot iconic landmarks like Tokyo Skytree. Arrive early to avoid crowds and enjoy clear visibility. Free entry.
Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden— Explore diverse garden styles (English, French, Japanese) in a serene oasis. Admire traditional landscaping and seasonal flowers. Entry fee: ¥500.

☀️Afternoon

Shinjuku Architecture Walk— Discover unique building designs, including Mode Gakuen Cocoon Tower. Observe modern architectural marvels and their integration with urban spaces. Free to walk.
Omoide Yokocho (Memory Lane)— Experience a nostalgic alleyway with tiny eateries. Sample affordable yakitori and ramen. Embrace the retro atmosphere and local flavors. Budget: ¥1000-¥2000.

🌙Evening

Godzilla Head at Hotel Gracery— Witness the iconic Godzilla head atop Hotel Gracery. Take photos with this pop-culture landmark. It's a fun and memorable experience. Free to view from outside.
Dinner at Hanbey Shinjuku Nishiguchi— Retro themed Izakaya restaurant. Enjoy cheap drinks and food while admiring the Showa era decor. Budget friendly and fun atmosphere. Budget: ¥2000-¥3000
💡 Travel Tip
Purchase a Suica or Pasmo card for easy and affordable travel on Tokyo's public transportation.
2
Day 2 - Asakusa & Tokyo Skytree
📍 View Asakusa, Tokyo on Map

🌅Morning

Senso-ji Temple— Explore Tokyo's oldest temple, Senso-ji, and its vibrant Nakamise-dori market. Experience traditional architecture and local crafts. Free entry to the temple.
Asakusa Architecture— Walk around the area and notice the traditional Japanese architecture. Visit the Asakusa Culture Tourist Information Center which has amazing architecture. Free.

☀️Afternoon

Sumida River Cruise (Optional)— Enjoy a scenic river cruise offering views of Tokyo's skyline. Admire the bridges and waterfront architecture. Budget: ¥1200-¥1500 (optional).
Tokyo Skytree Base Exploration— Explore the Solamachi shopping complex at the base of Tokyo Skytree. Enjoy window shopping and admire the tower's architecture. Free to explore.

🌙Evening

Tokyo Skytree Illumination (View from afar)— Observe the illuminated Tokyo Skytree from a distance. Enjoy the tower's dazzling light displays. Free viewing from the Sumida River area.
Dinner at Daikokuya Tempura— Enjoy a delicious and affordable Tempura Donburi. A local favorite, the restaurant is very popular. Budget: ¥1000-¥2000
💡 Travel Tip
Asakusa can get crowded, especially on weekends. Visit early in the morning for a more peaceful experience.
3
Day 3 - Shibuya & Harajuku
📍 View Shibuya, Tokyo on Map

🌅Morning

Shibuya Crossing— Experience the world's busiest intersection. Observe the organized chaos and capture iconic photos. Free and exhilarating experience. Best views from Starbucks.
Hachiko Statue— Visit the famous Hachiko statue, a symbol of loyalty. Learn about the heartwarming story behind this beloved landmark. Free to visit.

☀️Afternoon

Harajuku Takeshita Street— Explore Harajuku's vibrant Takeshita Street, known for its unique fashion and street style. Observe the colorful and eccentric trends. Budget for snacks.
Meiji Jingu Shrine— Find tranquility at Meiji Jingu Shrine, a peaceful oasis dedicated to Emperor Meiji and Empress Shoken. Free entry, donations welcome. Serene atmosphere.

🌙Evening

Shibuya Sky (Optional - Splurge)— Enjoy panoramic views of Tokyo from Shibuya Sky observation deck. A splurge option for breathtaking cityscapes. Budget: ¥2500 (optional).
Dinner at Genki Sushi Shibuya— Enjoy affordable and fun conveyor belt sushi. Try a variety of flavors and styles. Great for a quick and budget friendly dinner. Budget: ¥1000-¥2000.
💡 Travel Tip
Harajuku is a great place to people-watch. Be respectful when taking photos of people's unique styles.
4
Day 4 - Ginza & Imperial Palace East Garden
📍 View Ginza, Tokyo on Map

🌅Morning

Ginza Architecture Walk— Admire the upscale architecture of Ginza, including landmark department stores and designer boutiques. Observe the elegant and modern designs. Free to walk.
Kabuki-za Theatre (Exterior)— Admire the traditional architecture of the Kabuki-za Theatre. Consider attending a performance (budget permitting). Free to view the exterior.

☀️Afternoon

Imperial Palace East Garden— Explore the Imperial Palace East Garden, a historic site with beautiful gardens and remnants of Edo Castle. Free entry and peaceful atmosphere.
Marunouchi Naka-dori Street— Wander along Marunouchi Naka-dori Street, a stylish avenue with European-style architecture. Enjoy the ambiance and window shopping. Free to walk.

🌙Evening

Ginza Crossing at Night— Experience the illuminated Ginza crossing at night. Observe the vibrant lights and upscale atmosphere. Free and visually appealing.
Dinner at Yurakucho Gado Shita— Dine at Yurakucho Gado Shita, a network of restaurants under the train tracks. Enjoy affordable eats and a local atmosphere. Budget: ¥2000-¥3000
💡 Travel Tip
Ginza is known for its high-end shopping, but it's also a great place to admire architecture and enjoy a sophisticated atmosphere for free.
5
Day 5 - Ueno Park & Yanaka
📍 View Ueno Park, Tokyo on Map

🌅Morning

Ueno Park Exploration— Explore Ueno Park, a large public park with museums, temples, and a zoo. Enjoy the green spaces and cultural attractions. Free to enter the park.
Tokyo National Museum (Exterior)— Admire the architecture of the Tokyo National Museum, showcasing Japanese art and history. Consider visiting inside (budget permitting). Free to view exterior.

☀️Afternoon

Yanaka Ginza Shopping Street— Wander through Yanaka Ginza, a traditional shopping street with local shops and eateries. Sample street food and browse for souvenirs. Budget for snacks.
Yanaka Cemetery— Walk through Yanaka Cemetery, a peaceful and historic cemetery with beautiful trees and traditional Japanese graves. Free and serene atmosphere.

🌙Evening

Nezu Shrine— Visit Nezu Shrine, known for its vermilion torii gates and beautiful gardens. Experience traditional Shinto architecture. Free entry and peaceful atmosphere.
Dinner at Isego Honten— Enjoy a farewell dinner at Isego Honten, known for its delicious tempura. A great place to enjoy a traditional meal. Budget: ¥2000-¥3000
💡 Travel Tip
Ueno Park is a great place to spend a leisurely day exploring different cultural attractions. Check opening hours and admission fees for museums.
